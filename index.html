About

A short introduction.

I'm a Mechanical Engineering graduate from Manuel S. Enverga University Foundation. Right now I work as a freelance workflow automation specialist, and before that I worked as an AI researcher and developer on a range of client projects.

I'm comfortable working on both the software and hardware side of things. Most of my work is about taking a manual or repetitive process and making it automated and reliable. That might be an AI agent, a CRM setup, or an automation that connects a few tools together.

Outside of client work, I spend my time building drones, testing AI agents, and trying out new automation tools.
